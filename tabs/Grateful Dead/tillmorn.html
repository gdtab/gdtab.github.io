These chords are right out of the GD published sheet music for 
American Beauty.  I personally disagree with the E7+9, and not 
just because it's a bear to get to if you're not used to it.  It 
just doesn't sound right.  I think the E9 sounds better, or you 
can just play an E7.  The A7 is listed as it is because if you 
listen the disc, you'll hear them pick out notes.  If you play 
the A7 in this form, those notes are right there.  The E9, as 
listed in the song, is a must.  Lastly, if perfection is not a 
necessity, I think you can get away with playing a B7 instead 
of the B7+5.  Good luck.
