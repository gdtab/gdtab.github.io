The melody line is mixed in among the chords and the trick is getting
the melody note to stand out over the rest of the chord by either
hitting that string a little harder, or omitting the notes on the
higher strings so they don't drown it out. Play around with it, that's
what I do. I tried to approximate the strumming under te melody as
best i could --  You know the groove, though, it has a slow shuffle
feel, with the bass at 1 and 3 and the chords on the 2 beat and right
before three if you think of it in terms of 4/4 the idea is to get as
many of those in as you can without sacrificing the melody line.
